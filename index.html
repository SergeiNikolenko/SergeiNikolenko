I build scientific ML systems for molecular discovery: molecular docking, protein-ligand modeling, generative molecular design, ADMET/QSAR, chemical LLMs, spectra workflows, benchmarks, and reproducible Python/HPC infrastructure.

My work connects research models with usable software: evaluation pipelines, CLI/TUI tools, remote GPU/HPC workflows, and molecular-data tooling for computational chemistry teams.

Selected projects

Matcha

Multi-stage Riemannian flow matching for physically valid molecular docking, with GNINA scoring, pose filtering, CLI inference, and benchmarking workflows.

Stack: Python, PyTorch, RDKit, GNINA, PoseBusters, Typer, HPC

HEDGEHOG

Stage-based evaluation pipeline for generative molecular design: filters, retrosynthesis checks, docking, pose validation, reports, and CLI/TUI workflows.

Stack: Python, RDKit, AiZynthFinder, GNINA, SMINA, HTML reports

Bento

Reproducible benchmark for protein-ligand docking with curated annotations, pocket similarity, and HPC workflows.

Stack: Python, RDKit, uv, pytest, SLURM

SynthLadder

Chemistry benchmark and evaluation package for agentic LLMs across reaction understanding, retrosynthesis, route planning, and MS/MS tasks.

Stack: Python, RDKit, structured evaluation, LLM-as-judge

posecheck-fast

High-throughput docking pose validation with symmetry-corrected RMSD and lightweight distance and clash filters.

Stack: Python, RDKit, spyrmsd, PyTorch, PoseBusters

Burrete

macOS molecular preview tooling with Finder and Quick Look integration, Mol* visualization, fast XYZ rendering, and RDKit-powered compound grids.

Stack: Swift, macOS, Quick Look, WebKit, Mol*, RDKit.js, TypeScript
